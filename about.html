About ProFix Home Services

Serving Austin families with integrity since 2012

Built on Trust Since 2012

ProFix Home Services was founded in 2012 by Mike Johnson, a third-generation tradesman who grew up watching his father and grandfather fix homes across Central Texas. After 15 years working for large contractors, Mike saw an opportunity to build something better — a company that treats every home like its own.

What started as a one-man operation with a single truck has grown into a team of over 50 skilled technicians serving Austin and the surrounding communities. We've completed more than 8,500 jobs, earning a 4.9-star Google rating along the way.

Our mission is simple: deliver honest, high-quality home services at fair prices, with the kind of respect and reliability that keeps families coming back.

Our Values

The principles that guide everything we do.

Integrity

We provide honest assessments, transparent pricing, and never recommend work you don't need. Our reputation is built on trust — and we intend to keep it that way.

Quality

Every job meets our rigorous quality standards. We use premium materials, employ certified technicians, and stand behind our work with a 1-year warranty.

Reliability

We show up on time, every time. Our technicians respect your schedule, your home, and your budget. If we're late, your service call is free.
